How you can use spacing

Examples

Hello, Bulma

class="py-5 mt-5">

Adds 1.5rem of padding to top and bottom and 1.5rem of margin to top

Hello, Bulma

class="px-6 mx-6 py-6"

Adds 3rem of padding left and right, 3rem of margin left and right and 3rem padding top and bottom

Hello, Bulma

class="mt-4 pb-6 pl-3">

Adds 1rem of margin top, 3rem of padding bottom and 0.75rem of padding left padding left

Shortcuts

  • mt for margin top
  • mb for margin bottom
  • ml for margin left
  • mr for margin right
  • mx for margin left and right
  • my for margin top and bottom

    • Sizes

    Sizes Value
    0 0
    1 0.25rem
    2 0.5rem
    3 0.75rem
    4 1rem
    5 1.5rem
    6 3rem

    You can use a maximum of 3rem with margin and padding

    Shortcuts

  • pt for padding top
  • pb for padding bottom
  • pl for padding left
  • pr for padding right
  • px for padding left and right
